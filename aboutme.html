Hello, I'm Ali!

I'm a third-year computer science major from Dallas. I currently attend Texas A&M University, and my involvement with campus has been quite diverse, including leadership development, philanthropy work, Greek life, and music.

Outside of coding, my passions and pastimes involve photography, music, cars, and sneaker trade. The underlying technology behind these interests is the foundation of my career interest and swayed me into changing my field of study during my second year of university.
